Time Is Life

Group project together with 4 other students (3 artists and 2 programmers, myself included),
testing our project management and teamwork skills while developing a game.

Project Size

12 weeks 10 hours a week, 4 team members

Tools & Languages

Unity, C#, Perforce, Hack'n'Plan

Discription

Time Is Life is a fast-paced bullet hell dungeon crawler in which you have to defeat the worst enemy of all: The Time!

Master the skill of dodging and fighting your way through the maze of a haunted hotel scene to find and collect all the hidden treasures that will boost your score. Compete for the highest score in this Arcade-like vertical slice and get lost in the beautifully haunting atmosphere.

My Principal Contributions

Enemy Behavior

I designed all enemy logic of the game (pursue patterns, range definition, attack modes, etc...)

Bullet Patterns

We wanted a lot of enemy's with different attack patterns. Therefore I designed and coded an a system with a user friendly interface that enables the creation off attack patterns for each enemy by every member of our development team. For example: the amount of bullets, the number of bullets per cycle, time interval between cycles, the bullet spawn areas, etc...

Arena Rooms

Boss arena's, imprisoning the player in a room with a bunch of enemy's he needs to defeat.

Player Feedback

In the last few weeks of development I integrated player feedback, mostly sound effects and minor animations.
